Kadi is passionate about the DevOps / DevSecOps community since her days of working with COBOL development and Mainframe solutions. At  ReversingLabs she collaborates with developers and security researchers to educate to the open source community on software supply chain security. She previously hosted Wicked Good Development and now hosts 10x Insights- a podcast about the future of open source. When she's not working with the developer community, she loves running, traveling, and playing with her dog Milo.
