Darren Manners is a veteran cybersecurity architect, offensive security leader, and platform builder with more than two decades of experience operating at the intersection of advanced threat simulation, incident response, and autonomous security systems.

Darren has led the design and delivery of large-scale offensive security and continuous testing platforms used by enterprises to validate real-world risk — not just theoretical compliance. His work spans penetration testing, red teaming, adversary emulation, cloud security, and the development of agent-driven security orchestration systems that move beyond alerting into autonomous action.

Holding elite industry certifications including CCIE Security, SANS GSE, OSCP, CRTP, and GPEN, Darren is known for translating complex technical concepts into practical, operational strategies for security leaders. He has built and advised security programs across global enterprises, critical infrastructure, and highly regulated environments.

Today, Darren focuses on the emerging shift toward agentic AI in cybersecurity, exploring how autonomous offensive and defensive agents can continuously test, detect, and respond to threats at machine speed. His work challenges traditional SOC models and advocates for self-driving security operations designed to outpace modern adversaries.
