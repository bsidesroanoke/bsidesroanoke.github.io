Jeff Hoge is a Senior Cybersecurity Engineer at Echelon Risk + Cyber. Jeff has worked in the technology space for 18 years, including network engineering and systems administration. Most recently, he implemented and led the information security program at a regional healthcare insurer. His passion is helping individuals and organizations better defend themselves from cyber threats with an empathetic, relationship-oriented approach. Jeff currently serves as the Vice-President of the Triad of NC ISSA chapter and loves spending time with his four children.
