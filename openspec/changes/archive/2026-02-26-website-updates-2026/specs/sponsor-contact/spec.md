## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Sponsor contact email updated
The system SHALL display the sponsor contact email as sponsors@roanokeinfosec.com in the sponsorship section.

#### Scenario: Sponsor contact shows new email
- **WHEN** a user views the sponsors section of an event page
- **AND** they see the sponsorship inquiry text
- **THEN** the email address displayed SHALL be sponsors@roanokeinfosec.com
- **AND** it SHALL NOT be info@bsidesroanoke.org or any other email address
