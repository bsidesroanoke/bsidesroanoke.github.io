## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Blog post announces ticket availability with excitement
The system SHALL display a blog post announcing that tickets for BSides Roanoke 2026 are available for purchase using excited, engaging language and emojis to draw attention.

#### Scenario: Blog post uses excited language
- **WHEN** a user visits the blog page
- **THEN** they SHALL see a blog post with an exciting title indicating tickets are available
- **AND** the content SHALL use enthusiastic language and emojis to keep the user engaged

### Requirement: Blog post has clear call-to-action button
The blog post SHALL contain a prominent, easy-to-see button linking to the ticket purchase portal with action-oriented text.

#### Scenario: Ticket button is prominent and actionable
- **WHEN** a user views the blog post
- **THEN** they SHALL see a clear button/link to purchase tickets
- **AND** the button SHALL use action-oriented language (e.g., "Get Your Tickets Now!", "Grab Your Spot!")
- **AND** the button SHALL link to https://www.zeffy.com/en-US/ticketing/bsides-roanoke--2026

### Requirement: Blog post links to Zeffy ticketing
The blog post SHALL contain a direct link to the Zeffy ticketing portal for purchasing tickets.

#### Scenario: Ticket link navigates to purchase page
- **WHEN** a user clicks the ticket button/link in the blog post
- **THEN** they SHALL be navigated to the Zeffy ticketing page for BSides Roanoke 2026
